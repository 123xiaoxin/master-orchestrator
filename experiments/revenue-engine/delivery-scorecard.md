# Delivery Scorecard

Use this scorecard before sending any revenue experiment output to a real person.

| Criterion | Pass Condition | Score |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Buyer clarity | One specific buyer segment is named | 0-2 |
| Pain clarity | Pain points are concrete and observable | 0-2 |
| Deliverable clarity | The buyer can understand exactly what they receive | 0-2 |
| Scope control | Boundaries and exclusions are explicit | 0-2 |
| Evidence quality | Claims are sourced or marked as assumptions | 0-2 |
| Safety | No device control, secrets, or unsupported compliance claims | 0-2 |
| Reuse | Output can become a template, checklist, or sample | 0-2 |
| Sales readiness | A human can review and send the outreach draft | 0-2 |

Maximum score: 16.

## Gate

- 13-16: Ready for human final review.
- 9-12: Revise before use.
- 0-8: Do not use externally.

## Automatic Failure

Fail the deliverable immediately if it:

- Promises guaranteed income or ROI.
- Claims verified case results without evidence.
- Provides unsafe industrial operation instructions.
- Requests or exposes credentials.
- Sends or schedules outreach without human approval.
